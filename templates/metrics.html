Technology & Metrics

Our model is based off of Microsoft's TrOCR, an encoder-decoder model with a vision transformer encoder and a classic text transformer decoder.


We fine-tuned our model on the IAM Handwriting Database to improve its performance on handwritten text.
With a training length of 3 epochs, we were able to reduce our character error rate to 8.6%.
