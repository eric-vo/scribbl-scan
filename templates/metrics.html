Technology and Metrics

The core of our handwriting recognition technology is Microsoft's TrOCR, an encoder-decoder model that uses a vision Transformer as an encoder and a classic text Transformer as a decoder. Using a text Transformer as a decoder allows TrOCR to leverage an understanding of natural language in its transcriptions.

We fine-tune TrOCR on the IAM Handwriting Database to improve its performance on handwritten text. By training for 3 epochs using HuggingFace's Transformers library, we were able to achieve a character error rate of only 8.6%.
