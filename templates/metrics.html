Technology & Metrics

Our model is based off of Microsoft's TrOCR model, an encoder-decoder model with a vision transformer encoder and a classic text transformer decoder. Using a text Transformer as a decoder allows TrOCR to leverage an understanding of natural language in its transcriptions.


We fine-tuned our model on the IAM Handwriting Database to improve its performance on handwritten text.
With a training length of 3 epochs, we were able to reduce our character error rate to 8.6%, our training loss to 0.29, and our evaluation loss to 0.8.
